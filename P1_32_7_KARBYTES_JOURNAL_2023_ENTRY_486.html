I watched the starry night sky gradually fade into an increasingly bright blue dome as night faded into early morning from my vantage at the base of some hills covered in dry “white gold” grass, green shrubs, and green oak trees. I took a photograph of the scene with my Android phone and uploaded that image to Instagram and GitHub. Existence continues.
